About Web ML

A simple site that tries to classify images, like the one you're about to see.
Our goal is to help use computers in order to classify images for whatever you may need them classified for.


How to use

  1. Wait for the model to load.
  2. Choose an image.
  3. Press classify button and wait

How does it work?

Web ML uses something called a convolutional neural network
That means the computer breaks the image its given into small little pieces and compares them to pretrained model, possibly capable of recognizing the image in seconds.
